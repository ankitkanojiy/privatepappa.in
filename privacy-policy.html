Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Overview

PrivateVault is designed as an offline-first encrypted vault app. The app is intended to protect files locally on your device and does not intentionally collect, sell, transmit, upload, or share your personal vault contents.

Data Collection

In the intended offline production configuration, PrivateVault does not collect user data through analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking SDKs, cloud sync, or remote servers.

The app does not intentionally collect:

  • photos, videos, audio, or documents stored in your vault
  • vault passwords or encryption keys
  • contacts, messages, call logs, or location
  • advertising identifiers or behavioral analytics
  • usage tracking or crash analytics

Permissions and File Access

PrivateVault requests only permissions needed for user-selected app features. Media and file access permissions are used to import, preview, encrypt, decrypt, export, or manage files that you choose.

  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES may be used for selected image imports and previews.
  • READ_MEDIA_VIDEO may be used for selected video imports and previews.
  • READ_MEDIA_AUDIO may be used for selected audio imports and previews where enabled.
  • Android file picker access may be used so you can choose documents or folders.
  • CAMERA may be requested only when you choose direct camera capture or document scan features.

Core vault operation is designed not to require internet permission.

How Data Is Used

Files you select are used locally to provide vault features, including encryption, decryption, local preview, local thumbnails, local search, export, sharing, and organization.

Vault data is not sent to the developer by the app in the intended offline configuration.

Sharing and Disclosure

PrivateVault does not intentionally share your vault contents with third parties. Files may leave the app only when you explicitly export or share decrypted files using your device’s storage picker or Android share sheet.

Security

The app is designed with local encryption, secure vault sessions, protected previews, screenshot blocking on sensitive screens, temporary file cleanup, and Android platform security features where supported. No app can guarantee absolute security in every environment, so users should keep their devices updated and use strong passwords.

Children’s Privacy

PrivateVault is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Safety Summary

  • Data sold: No
  • Data shared by default: No
  • Data collected by developer servers: No, in the intended offline configuration
  • Cloud sync: No mandatory cloud sync
  • Ads or tracking SDKs: Not intentionally included in the offline vault configuration

Policy Updates

This policy may be updated to reflect app changes, permission changes, Play Console requirements, or legal requirements.
