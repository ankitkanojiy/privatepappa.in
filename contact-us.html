Offline Privacy Note

Because PrivateVault and PrivateVault PRO operate as fully offline encrypted environments on your device, all data and encryption keys stay strictly local. The developer does not run any cloud servers, meaning we have no way to access your vault, reset passwords, or recover lost files.

Support Guidance

When asking for support, include your app version, Android version, device model, and a clear description of the issue. Do not send vault passwords or private files.
