Moving from PrivateVault to PrivateVault PRO

To move your vault data to PrivateVault PRO, create a password-protected encrypted backup in PrivateVault and save it to a location of your choice on your device. Then open PrivateVault PRO, choose Restore, select that backup file, and enter the same backup password. Your data remains fully offline, encrypted, and in your control throughout the process with no cloud transfer required.

Limitations

PrivateVault does not guarantee that every file type will preview or render perfectly. Unsupported, corrupted, extremely large, unusual, encrypted, or password-protected files may fail to open, preview, or process.

Scan-to-PDF quality may depend on image quality, lighting, document angle, and device limitations.

No software can guarantee that encrypted files will never become corrupted. Users should maintain independent backups of important files. If a vault password is forgotten or lost, encrypted files may be permanently inaccessible — at least no person on Earth will be able to recover those files without the correct password.
