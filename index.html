Why Private Photos?

A photo vault should feel like a vault — not a sync service in disguise.

AES-256-GCM Encryption

Every byte of every photo is wrapped in authenticated encryption. Tampered files are rejected automatically.

100% Offline

No internet permission requested. Nothing leaves your phone — not even crash reports.

Master-Password Only

Your password derives the encryption key with Argon2 / PBKDF2. We can’t reset it because we never see it.

Screenshot-Proof

The vault sets Android’s FLAG_SECURE, blocking screenshots, screen recording, and recent-apps previews.

Tamper-Detection

Offline certificate pinning verifies the APK signature on every launch. Modded builds wipe themselves.

Encrypted Backups

Export the entire vault as a single sealed file. Restore on any device with your password — nothing else.

Fast Native Codec

Streamed encryption pipeline encrypts thousands of photos without freezing the UI or filling RAM.

Made in India
