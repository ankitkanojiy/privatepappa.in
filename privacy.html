Privacy Policy

The shortest, most accurate privacy policy we could write: we collect nothing.

Last updated: January 2025  ·  Effective immediately

TL;DR — Private Photos has no servers, no accounts and no internet permission. It cannot collect, transmit, or store your data anywhere outside your own device.

1. Who we are

“We,” “us,” and “the Developer” refer to privatepappa.in, the publisher and developer of the Android application “Private Photos” (the “App”). Our website is https://privatepappa.in. Questions about this policy can be sent to reply@privatepappa.in.

2. Information we collect

None. The App does not collect, transmit, sell, share or process any personal information, photos, or telemetry. Specifically:

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, IP address, advertising ID, or any other identifier.
  • We do not collect your photos, videos, file names, EXIF data, or any metadata about them.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs (Firebase Analytics, Crashlytics, Mixpanel, etc.).
  • We do not use ad networks or tracking pixels.
  • We do not request the Android INTERNET permission. The App cannot open a network socket.

3. Information stored on your device

The App stores the following only on your device, in its app-private storage area:

  • Encrypted photo blobs — the photos you choose to import, each encrypted byte-by-byte using AES-256-GCM with a key derived from your master password.
  • An encrypted index — a small file mapping blobs back to thumbnails and ordering. It is encrypted with the same key.
  • A password verifier — a non-reversible Argon2 / PBKDF2 hash used solely to check whether the password you typed is correct. The password itself is never stored.
  • App preferences — theme, sort order, etc. No personal data.

This data never leaves your device unless you deliberately export it via the App’s backup feature.

4. Permissions explained

  • Photos & Media (READ_MEDIA_IMAGES, READ_MEDIA_VIDEO) — needed to import photos from your gallery into the vault. Used only when you tap “Add photos.”
  • Biometric (USE_BIOMETRIC) — optional, used only if you enable fingerprint / face unlock. Biometric data never leaves the system keystore.
  • Storage (scoped, Android 11+) — used for backup export and restore, only when you initiate it.

We do not request: Internet, location, contacts, microphone, camera (unless you tap Capture), background activity, or system overlays.

5. Third-party services

The App does not embed third-party SDKs that collect data. There are no advertising libraries, no analytics libraries, no remote-config libraries, no crash reporters that report off-device.

If we ever integrate an optional service in a future version (for example, an opt-in donation processor), it will be explicitly disclosed here before release, and you will be asked to consent before any data leaves your device.

6. Children’s privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13. Since we collect nothing, no special procedures are required.

7. Data retention & deletion

Since we don’t hold any data on our servers, there is nothing for us to retain or delete on our side. To delete data on your device, simply uninstall the App. Android will permanently wipe the App’s private storage — including the encrypted vault — as part of the uninstall.

8. Security

  • AES-256-GCM authenticated encryption on every file.
  • Argon2 / PBKDF2 key derivation with a high work factor to resist brute-force.
  • Offline certificate pinning — the App verifies its own signing certificate on launch and refuses to run if the APK has been tampered with.
  • Screen-capture protection via Android FLAG_SECURE.
  • Sandboxed storage — the vault lives in app-private storage, inaccessible to other apps on a non-rooted device.

9. Your rights

Under the EU GDPR, the California CCPA, India’s DPDP Act and most other privacy frameworks, you have the right to access, correct, port and delete your personal data held by a controller. Since we hold none of your personal data, these requests are honored by default — there is simply nothing to retrieve or erase on our side.

If you believe the App has somehow collected data from you, please email hello@privatepappa.in and we will investigate within 7 working days.

10. Changes to this policy

If our practices change — for example, if a future paid version uses an optional payment processor — we will update this page and bump the “Last updated” date. Material changes will also be announced inside the App on the next launch.
